Meet The Team

Andy was raised in the hair industry in his Mothers salon where his older sisters worked. When he opened Made Barbers he wanted to create a relaxing and friendly environment where people could get a haircut that they would love. Even though he never shuts up he still somehow manages to put a smile on everybody's face.

David has been described as courageous, handsome, whitty all with a genius intellect. David has been described this by David of course which all of us completely ignore. He has however grown into an amazing barber since joining our team which is why he is the manager of our shop. He was the first barber Andy hired and the two have been pushing each others buttons ever since!

Martin is the newest member of Made. He studied at the prestigious SB barbering academy, found an immediate love for barbering excelled quickly. He then joined the team to further his knowledge. After seeing his progress and being the absolute nerd he is, the guys had to make him part of the team and now you can catch him in the Titan shop.

Tim began his career as a hairdresser in 2004 in India. He moved to the UK in 2013 and attended beauty school to obtain his license as a professional hairdresser. Thereafter, he worked in a Japanese unisex salon until he decided to enter the barbering field by joining 18/8 Fine Men's Saloon in London. With 17 years of barbering experience, Tim has mastered the art of haircutting!

John is an expert barber and hairstylist, trained in France, who has been plying his trade in high-end salons since the 90s, styling both men and women. In the last few years, he has become specialized in men's grooming. John is especially fond of scissor cuts, as these allow him to adapt the cut to each person's individual style, hair type, and face shape.

Andy has been barbering for 36 years making him one of our most experienced barbers! His favourite cuts range from the classic and traditional city gents looks to modern textured looks. Andy is fantastic at understanding what someone really wants from a haircut, making him popular with his long-term clients. Andy also offers an exclusive colouring service.
